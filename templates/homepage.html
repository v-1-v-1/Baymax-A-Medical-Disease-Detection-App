About

Baymax - Your Personal Healthcare Companion is a state-of-the-art AI-driven medical platform designed to revolutionize healthcare by addressing the urgent need for comprehensive disease detection. Focusing on seven major illnesses, including Covid-19, Brain Tumor, and Breast Cancer, Baymax utilizes advanced machine learning models to achieve accurate diagnostics. The primary goal is to provide immediate, home-based test results through a user-friendly interface, fostering a proactive approach to healthcare in our rapidly evolving world. Baymax aims to be a trusted companion, bridging the gap between AI advancements and medical practice, thereby transforming diagnostics and contributing to the evolution of modern healthcare.
